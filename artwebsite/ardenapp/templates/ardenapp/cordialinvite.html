{% load static %} A Cordial Invite

You are cordially invited!



Your divine presence has been requested

At the cheery Christoval estate of the Neff family,

To endeavor in this season's bountiful grape harvest.


The head of estate has decreed the affair to take place

On Friday and Saturday, August 8th-9th.


Although the hours be long and the work perhaps tiresome,

the young maiden of the Neff family endeavors to entice thee

with room and board, and the promise to share the secrets

of her homeland.


Note that the dates are yet to be carved in stone,

and the vines may wish to be plucked Saturday-Sunday,

or perhaps still some other time, which will soon be discerned more fully.
